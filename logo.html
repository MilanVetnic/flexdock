What services does FlexDock provide?

FlexDock LLC provides cross docking, storage, loading and unloading, and final-mile delivery across the DFW metroplex — plus 24/7 operations and restock support to keep your supply chain moving.

02
Are you really open 24/7?

Yes. Day or night, we keep your supply chain moving.

Our dock runs around the clock, so your freight never waits. Always on. Always moving. Always ready.

03
How do you ensure on-time delivery?

We minimize handling and maximize speed.

Cross-dock speed plus a team that knows the DFW area means your freight arrives on time, every time — across Fort Worth, Dallas, and beyond.

What is cross docking, and how does it lower my costs?

Cross docking moves freight straight from inbound to outbound with little or no storage time.

Less handling, less storage, faster flow — and lower costs across your supply chain.

With quick-turnaround restock available 24/7, we keep your shelves full and your business growing.
