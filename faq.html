What services does FlexDock LLC provide?

FlexDock LLC handles cross-docking, secure storage, freight loading and unloading, and final-mile delivery.

Faster flow. Lower costs. We do the hands so you can grow your business.

We serve the entire Dallas–Fort Worth (DFW) metroplex, including Fort Worth, Dallas, Denton, McKinney, Plano, Arlington, and Waxahachie.

Delivering results across DFW.

What is cross-docking, and why does it matter?

Cross-docking moves your freight straight from inbound to outbound with little or no storage time.

Minimize handling. Maximize speed. It cuts transit time and shipping costs while keeping your supply chain moving.

Do you offer storage and restock support?

Yes. We provide secure, flexible warehouse space for your inventory, plus 24/7 restock support.

Quick turnaround to keep your shelves full.
