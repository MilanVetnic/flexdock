Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
At FlexDock LLC, your privacy — and the security of your data — matters deeply to us.

We believe that trust begins with transparency. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website, services, or communication channels.
1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide accurate, secure, and efficient logistics services.

This may include:

  • Information you provide directly (name, company, email address, phone number, inquiry details).
  • Operational data you share when requesting quotes, booking shipments, or contacting our team.
  • Website analytics, collected anonymously, to help us understand how visitors use our website (e.g., which pages are visited, how long users stay, and what regions traffic comes from).

We do not collect unnecessary personal data, and we do not sell, rent, or share information for advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and prepare quotes or service proposals.
  • Manage logistics operations, customer communication, and support.
  • Improve our website, platform usability, and client experience.
  • Maintain accurate internal records for compliance and service delivery.

We never use your data in ways unrelated to these operational purposes.

3. Cookies and Analytics

The FlexDock LLC website uses limited cookies to improve site performance and measure general traffic analytics.

These cookies do not identify individual users and are never used for advertising. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. We use only secure, industry-standard analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics).

4. Data Security

All personal and operational data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized FlexDock LLC personnel who require it to perform their duties.

We use encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. Data is retained only as long as necessary for operational, contractual, or legal purposes.

5. Your Rights

As a FlexDock LLC client or website user, you have the right to:

This may include:

  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Request corrections or deletion of data we hold about you.
  • Withdraw consent for communication at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at flexdockllc@gmail.com. We will respond promptly and transparently.

6. Third-Party Services

FlexDock LLC partners only with trusted, compliant service providers who help us deliver our logistics operations and website services, including:

  • Web hosting, analytics, and performance monitoring tools.
  • Communication systems and customer management software.

All partners meet current data protection standards and are prohibited from using FlexDock LLC data for their own marketing or unrelated purposes.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect operational improvements or legal requirements. Any significant changes will be communicated through this page. The latest version is always available on this page.

8. Contact Us

FlexDock LLC is a cross-dock, warehousing, and final-mile logistics company based in Fort Worth, Texas, serving the Dallas–Fort Worth metroplex. Faster flow. Lower costs.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, reach out — we're always on, day or night.
